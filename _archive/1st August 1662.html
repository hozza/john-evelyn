--- source_date: 1st August 1662 date: 1662-08-01 ---

Mr. H. Howard, his brothers Charles, Edward, Bernard, Philip,73 now the Queen's Almoner (all brothers of the Duke of Norfolk, still in Italy), came with a great train, and dined with me; Mr. H. Howard leaving with me his eldest and youngest sons, Henry and Thomas, for three or four days, my son, John, having been sometime bred up in their father's house.
