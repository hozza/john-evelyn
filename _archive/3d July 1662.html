--- source_date: 3d July 1662 date: 1662-07-03 ---

My wife met me at Woodcot, whither Mr. Howard accompanied me to see my son John, who[Pg 360] had been much brought up among Mr. Howard's children at Arundel House, till, for fear of their perverting him in the Catholic religion, I was forced to take him home.

LONDON
