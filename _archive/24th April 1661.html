--- source_date: 24th April 1661 date: 1661-04-24 ---

I presented his Majesty with his "Panegyric"66 in the Privy Chamber, which he was pleased to accept most graciously; I gave copies to the Lord Chancellor, and most of the noblemen who came to me for it. I dined at the Marquis of Ormond's where was a magnificent feast, and many great persons.
