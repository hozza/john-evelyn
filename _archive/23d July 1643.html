The Covenant being pressed, I absented myself; but, finding it impossible to evade the doing very unhandsome things, and which had been a great cause of my perpetual motions hitherto between Wotton and London, October the 2d, I obtained a[Pg 39] license of his Majesty, dated at Oxford and signed by the King, to travel again.
