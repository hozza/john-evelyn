--- source_date: 1st September 1662 date: 1662-09-01 ---

Being invited by Lord Berkeley, I went to Durdans, where dined his Majesty, the Queen,[Pg 363] Duke, Duchess, Prince Rupert, Prince Edward, and abundance of noblemen. I went, after dinner, to visit my brother of Woodcot, my sister having been delivered of a son a little before, but who had now been two days dead.
