--- source_date: 18th August 1644 date: 1644-08-18 ---

The Queen of England came to Tours, having newly arrived in France, and going for Paris. She was very nobly received by the people and[Pg 73] clergy, who went to meet her with the trained bands. After the harangue, the Archbishop entertained her at his Palace, where I paid my duty to her. The 20th she set forward to Paris.
