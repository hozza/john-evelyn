--- source_date: 6th August 1657 date: 1657-08-06 ---

I went to see Colonel Blount, who showed me the application of the waywiser58 to a coach,[Pg 317] exactly measuring the miles, and showing them by an index as we went on. It had three circles, one pointing to the number of rods, another to the miles, by 10 to 1,000, with all the subdivisions of quarters; very pretty and useful.
