--- source_date: 4th April 1701 date: 1701-04-04 ---

The Duke of Norfolk died of an apoplexy, and Mr. Thomas Howard of complicated disease since his being cut for the stone; he was one of the Tellers of the Exchequer. Mr. How made a Baron.

May, 1701. Some Kentish men, delivering a petition to the House of Commons, were imprisoned.93

A great dearth, no considerable rain having fallen for some months.
