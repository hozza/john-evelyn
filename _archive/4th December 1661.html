--- source_date: 4th December 1661 date: 1661-12-04 ---

I had much discourse with the Duke of York, concerning strange cures he affirmed of a woman who swallowed a whole ear of barley, which worked out at her side. I told him of the KNIFE SWALLOWED69 and the pins.

I took leave of the Bishop of Cape Verd, now going in the fleet to bring over our new Queen.
